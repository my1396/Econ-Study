**Modale hjelpeverb** (modal verbs) 

## Skulle -- skal -- skulle -- har skullet

### A. Bestemmelse, plan

*(<span style="color: #00CC66;">Decided actions, plan, official or scheduled arrangement</span>)*

-   I morgen *skal* Kåre reise til Bodø.  
    → Tomorrow Kåre is going to travel to Bodø.
-   Jeg *skal* ringe deg senere.  
    → I will call you later.
-   Jeg *skal* studere i kveld.  
    → I am going to study tonight.
-   Møtet *skal* begynne klokka ti.  
    → The meeting is scheduled to start at ten o'clock.

### B. Påbud eller regel

*(Order, obligation, rule, requirement)*

-   Du *skal* sitte der!  
    → You must sit there!

-   Nå *skal* du høre på meg!  
    → Now you must listen to me!



### C. Oppfordring eller forslag

*(Invitation or suggestion)*

-   *Skal* vi gå på kino i kveld?  
    → Shall we go to the cinema tonight?

-   *Skal* vi danse?  
    → Shall we dance?

**Note:**
På C bruker vi ikke *skal* i svaret, men for eksempel:

-   Ja, det *kan* vi godt.  
    → Yes, we can do that.

> 回答 «skal» 引导的邀请或者建议时，不用 *skal*，而用 *kan*.

### D. Rykte, noe vi har hørt

*(Rumour, reported information)*

-   Hun *skal* være veldig rik.  
    → She is said to be very rich.

-   Mannen *skal* være syk.  
    → The man is said to be ill.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


## Ville -- vil -- ville -- villet


### A. Ønske eller lyst

*(<span style="color: #00CC66;">Wish or desire</span>; when combined with «ikke», meaning refusal)*

-   Hun *vil* ikke se ham mer.  
    → She does not want to see him anymore.

-   Jeg *vil* ha noe å drikke.  
    → I want something to drink.

-   Jeg *vil* hjelpe deg.  
    → I want to help you.

-   Han *vil* ikke gå på skolen i dag.  
    → He does not want to go to school today.

-   Han *vil* ikke høre.  
    → He does not want to listen.

### B. Framtid 

*(<span style="color: #00CC66;">Prediction of future; what is expected to happen</span>)*

> 这个是最容易混淆的用法。直觉上，会想用 «skal» 来表示未来，但是 
> - «skal» 更多的是表示计划和安排，而 
> - «vil» 则是对未来的预测和期望。✅

-   Vi vet ikke hva framtiden *vil* bringe.  
    → We do not know what the future will bring.
-   Det *vil* bli kaldt i morgen.  
    → It will be cold tomorrow.
-   Trening *vil* forbedre helsen.  
    → Exercise will improve health.
-   Trening *vil* redusere risikoen for sykdom.  
    → Exercise will reduce the risk of illness.
