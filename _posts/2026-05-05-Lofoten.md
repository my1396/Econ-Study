## 买票

On this route, it is possible to pre-book and reserve a portion of the capacity per departure. If the desired departure is sold out, you can show up at the quay without reserving a place and queue up in the regular line. It is not possible to combine pedestrian and vehicle in the same reservation.

票价: 735 kr for a car, pessengers are free. (605 kr for ticket + 130 kr for reservation fee)

## 退票规则

Terms of Service for ticket purchase

These travel conditions apply when booking tickets on ferries in Torghatten Nord:

- You are responsible for presenting your Booking when boarding.
- If the Booking is not validated upon boarding, you risk having to purchase a new ticket.
- The Booking is only valid for the vehicle and/or persons it is booked for.
- No refund for guaranteed space in case of cancellation (No-Show).
- Park the vehicle in the area marked "Reserved".
- The Booking is only valid for the departure and date it is reserved for. Remember to arrive no later than 45 minutes before departure.
- The place guarantee cannot be refunded, and you can buy a maximum of 2 seat guarantees at the same time.
  
  It is not possible to combine pedestrians and vehicles in the same order.
- For cancellations at least 30 days before the journey: The ticket price minus the reservation fee will be refunded.
- For cancellations between 30 and 14 days before the journey: The portion of the ticket price refunded, will be gradually reduced to 50%.
- If the journey is closer than 14 days away: The ticket price will not be refunded.
