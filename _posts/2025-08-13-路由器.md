-   FIBER / SFP+ (光纤口): Used for connecting to fiber optic internet. 接入运营商光纤 (主网入口)
    
    SFP+ (Small Form-factor Pluggable) 接口不同于普通的以太网接口。普通网线使用 RJ45 接口，而 SFP+ 使用的是小型可插拔模块接口。
    SFP+ 模块可以支持不同类型的连接，如光纤或铜缆，提供更高的带宽和更远的传输距离。

-   USB: Used for WAN backup, file sharing and media server. Not in use.
-   Phone 1 and Phone 2: Ports for connecting an analog telephone.
-   LAN 1-5: TV and broadband/internet.
    
    - 10G LAN: This is the port for internet in bridge mode. 
      
      超高速有线口 (适合高速宽带); 用于 NAS (网络硬盘), 高端电脑，10 Gbps 网络。
    
    - LAN 1-4: 普通家庭网口; 适合电视盒子，普通电脑，打印机，交换机等。

-   Power: The power adapter included in the box connects here.
-   Reset: Resets the home center. Use a paperclip or similar and hold for approx. 5 seconds. The network name and password are not deleted. The restart may take 2 to 3 minutes.
-   WPS: Press and hold for about 5 seconds to set up a wireless connection to a WPS-compatible device.
-   WLAN: Press and hold for about 2 seconds to activate wireless function.
